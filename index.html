    Concept

    The Tales of Annie is a 2D horror game in which players takes on the role of "Annie Harper". The game leads players to explore a house and solve puzzles with a storyline to immerse players in uncovering the truth about her grandmother.

    Story

    Due to the busy work schedule of her mother, "Charlotte Harper", she decides to leave Annie under the care of her grandmother in the countryside. However, Annie faces a mystical and weird experience since she arrives at the grandmother's house. This situation makes her to explore the truth by solving puzzles inside the house to find secrets hidden and Grandma's true side.

    Gameplay - Level design

    • Puzzle 1 (Living Room): The game begins in a cozy living room. Players must search carefully to find a hidden key, the only way to enter Grandma‚Äôs locked room and uncover her secret.
    • Puzzle 2 (Bookcase): Players receive a hint that refers to the living room‚Äôs dusty bookcase. Players must analyze subtle clues and piece together the truth behind Grandma‚Äôs tales.
